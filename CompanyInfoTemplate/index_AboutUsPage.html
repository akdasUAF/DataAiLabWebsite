About the lab

Who are we?

The Data/AI Lab at the University of Alaska Fairbanks is at the forefront of pioneering data-driven interdisciplinary research in the Last Frontier. Designed as a hub for innovation, the lab fosters collaboration among technology enthusiasts to develop cutting-edge solutions to real-world challenges. With a strong emphasis on machine learning, data analytics, and AI technologies, our mission is to push the boundaries of what's possible in computer science, transforming visionary ideas into impactful advancements. Join us as we lead the charge in shaping the future of technology and research in Alaska.

Our Mission

The mission of the Data/AI Lab at the University of Alaska Fairbanks is to drive transformative innovation through interdisciplinary research in data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. We are committed to advancing technology solutions that address real-world challenges, foster collaboration among diverse fields, and push the limits of what's possible in computer science. By empowering the next generation of researchers and innovators, we strive to lead the way in shaping a smarter, more connected future.

Our Vision

The vision of the Data/AI Lab at the University of Alaska Fairbanks is to become a global leader in cutting-edge research and innovation in data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence to address Arctic Challenges. We aim to empower interdisciplinary collaboration that drives impactful solutions in the polar and circumpolar region, advancing technology for the betterment of society in this region. By fostering a culture of creativity and exploration, we aspire to unlock new possibilities that shape the future of science, industry, and beyond, positioning Alaska at the forefront of the digital revolution.
