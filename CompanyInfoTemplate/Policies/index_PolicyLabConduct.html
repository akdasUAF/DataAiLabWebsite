Data & AI Laboratory Conduct Policy

Standards for Ethical, Secure, and Professional Data & AI Research

1. Purpose

This policy establishes standards for professional conduct, data stewardship, and ethical artificial intelligence practice within the Data & Artificial Intelligence Laboratory. The goal is to ensure a secure, collaborative, and responsible environment for research, development, and experimentation.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all individuals with access to the Data/AI Lab, including:

  • Faculty, principal investigators, and lab directors
  • Researchers, engineers, and analysts
  • Students, interns, and trainees
  • Visiting scholars, collaborators, and contractors

3. Professional Conduct

  • Act with professionalism, integrity, and respect
  • Communicate clearly and constructively
  • Maintain a collaborative and inclusive environment
  • Disclose conflicts of interest when they arise

Harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior—whether in person or online— will not be tolerated.

4. Data Governance and Stewardship

  • Use data only for approved and documented purposes
  • Comply with data use agreements, licenses, and institutional policies
  • Follow applicable data protection laws and regulations (e.g., GDPR, HIPAA, FERPA)
  • Store data securely using approved systems
  • Do not transfer sensitive data to unauthorized devices or services

All suspected data breaches or misuse must be reported immediately.

5. Ethical AI and Responsible Use

  • Align work with principles of fairness, transparency, accountability, and privacy
  • Avoid introducing or amplifying bias or harm
  • Document models, datasets, assumptions, and limitations
  • Prohibit use of AI systems for unlawful, deceptive, or harmful purposes
  • Comply with institutional guidelines for generative AI use and disclosure

6. Research Integrity and Reproducibility

  • Accurately document data sources, code, and experiments
  • Use version control and reproducible workflows when feasible
  • Do not fabricate, falsify, or selectively report results
  • Properly attribute intellectual contributions and sources

7. Computing Resources and Infrastructure

  • Use computing resources responsibly and efficiently
  • Do not share credentials or bypass security controls
  • Install only authorized software
  • Respect compute usage limits and allocation policies

8. Security and Confidentiality

  • Protect confidential data, models, and unpublished research
  • Use strong passwords and multi-factor authentication
  • Secure devices and workstations when unattended
  • Remain vigilant against phishing and social engineering

9. Collaboration and External Engagement

  • Obtain approval before sharing data, code, or models externally
  • Ensure collaborations comply with legal and ethical obligations
  • Respect open-source and licensing requirements

10. Training and Competency

  • Complete required training in data security and responsible AI
  • Maintain competency in tools and methodologies
  • Seek guidance when ethical or technical uncertainty exists

11. Violations and Enforcement

Violations of this policy may result in corrective action, including retraining, revocation of access, disciplinary measures, or legal action where applicable. Serious incidents will be escalated to appropriate authorities.

12. Acknowledgment

All lab members must acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agree to comply with this Data & AI Laboratory Conduct Policy as a condition of access and participation.
