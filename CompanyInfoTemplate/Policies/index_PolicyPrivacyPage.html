CCPA Privacy Rights (Do Not Sell My Personal Information)

Under the CCPA, among other rights, California consumers have the right to:

Request that a business that collects a consumer's personal data disclose the categories and specific pieces of personal data that a business has collected about consumers.

Request that a business delete any personal data about the consumer that a business has collected.

Request that a business that sells a consumer's personal data, not sell the consumer's personal data.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

Privacy notice for live synchronous lecture

Onstitute enables instructors to record, share, and upload lectures, including those recorded using Third Party Offerings, to Onstitute for their students to view on the students’ mobile devices or computers. In addition to the activities outlined elsewhere in our Privacy Notice, live synchronous lecture users are directed to review the following:
1. Onstitute will collect the audio and video content instructors choose to upload through Third Party Offerings, as well as the instructor’s name, email address, and thumbnail image. Instructors may also provide us with the contact information of their students to allow Onstitute to share the link to that content with those students. Instructors should ensure that the proper consents and permissions are obtained from these students before sharing their contact information. Please note that the audio and video content instructors provide to Onstitute may include the voices of students participating in the live lecture, and that we may make all such content publicly available to all users of our platform.
2. Onstitute will use your information to provide and operate our live synchronous lecture services and will make certain information available to other live synchronous lecture participants: For example, we will allow students to view the content uploaded by their instructors and will allow instructors to know which students have viewed their lectures.
3. Onstitute may also share certain information about your use of live synchronous lecture Services back with any associated Third Party Offering. Please note that when you interact with any Third Party Offering, such Third Party Offering may independently collect information about you and solicit information from you. We have no control over, do not review, and are not responsible for a Third Party Offering's privacy practices. We recommend that you review any and all Third Party Offering’s privacy notice for more information.
4. Onstitute strongly recommends not to share any personal information with any participants (instructors, students, and others) of the live synchronous lecture. Onstitute is not liable for any private information leaked verbally during the live synchronous lecture
