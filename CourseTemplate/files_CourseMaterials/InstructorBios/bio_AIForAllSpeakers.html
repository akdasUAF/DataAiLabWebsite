Orion Lawlor

Associate Professor of Computer Science,
University of Alaska Fairbanks

Dr. Orion Lawlor has been faculty at the UAF Computer Science department for 20 years, after earning his PhD in CS at UIUC in 2005, and Bachelor's degrees in CS and Math from UAF in 1999. His research interests are robotics, advanced manufacturing, remote sensing, and computer graphics.



Kevin Galloway

Linux and HPC Analyst at Research Computing System (RCS),
University of Alaska Fairbanks

Kevin Galloway has been with Research Computing Systems for almost 9 years, currently as a Linux and HPC Analyst. He received his Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Computer Science from UAF. His work focuses on system administering HPC clusters and helping researchers access and utilize high performance computing.
